Hangman Game

Instructions

To play, aim to decipher the concealed word before the hangman's figure is fully drawn. Begin by picking a category or difficulty level. A random word will be selected, depicted by dashes. Deduce letters one by one to uncover the mystery word.

Correct guesses will fill in the blanks, while incorrect ones will contribute to completing the hangman's illustration. Be mindful not to make too many incorrect guesses, as it will lead to the hangman's demise, concluding the game.
